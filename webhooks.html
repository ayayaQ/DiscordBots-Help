Webhooks

What can you do?

These allow you to send messages to a certain channel using the built-in webhooks integration on Discord. You can send either a regular message or an embed.

How do I create a webhook for my server?

Go to your desired server and click on "Server Settings", from there you click on "Integrations" and then "Create Webhook". This will create the webhook for you and all you need to do is copy the webhook URL to the Bot Commander app.
